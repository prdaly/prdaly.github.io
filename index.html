Automated Quadcopter with an Arduino Microcontroller

May 2022 - Present

I am currently developing an automated Arudino quadcopter made completely from scratch, with the goal of achieving autonomous hovering. I developed the control system using PID loops and Kalman Filters, integrating data from sensors such as a GPS, barometer, accelerometer, and gyroscope. I also designed and 3D-printed the frame, conducting simulations to optimize its structure and minimize weight. With the Arduino flight controller completed, I am currently working on refining the control loop to allow smooth and autonmous flight.

Train-of-Four Medical Device

Sep 2023 - Nov 2023

I worked on iterating a small handheld Train-of-Four (TOF) monitoring device, which is used to assess neuromuscular blockade levels in patients, typically under anesthesia. The device incorporates a small balloon under the thumb to measure muscle twitches, correlating to the patient's level of sedation. I developed ambidextrous prototypes, focusing on ergonomic design to improve usability across diverse hand sizes. Using a 3D handheld scanner and PLA 3D printing, I created three iterations with refined thumb and finger supports and tested their adaptability across various hand sizes.

WPI IQP - Analyzing Venetian Commerce

Aug 2022 - Dec 2022

As part of a travel abroad program, I worked with the WPI Venice Project Center to develop an application and database to track retail and commerce trends in Venice, Italy. We collected data on all 5,100+ Venetian storefronts and businesses.
