About

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Patrick Daly. I recently graduated from Worcester Polytechnic Institute in the spring of 2024, completing an accelerated BS and MS degree program in Mechanical Engineering in four years with high distinction. I have:

• Collaborated with Honeywell for my senior capstone MQP project to create an empirical model of Partial Emission Pumps for use in hydrogen fuel cell drones, awarded 5th out of 64 WPI Mechanical Engineering capstone projects.

• Completed an engineering internship at Collins Aerospace in San Dimas, CA, where I was worked to identify, diagnose, and resolve design-related quality issues and non-conformances on NASA Orion cooling systems and ISS particulate analyzers.

• Worked in positions supporting graduate level projects in multiple labs, including the Multiscale Heat Transfer Laboratory, Center for Advanced Research in Drying (CARD), and the Engineering Experimentation Lab.

I am passionate about product design and am seeking a position where I can contribute to product development from concept to completion.
